Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Donations

MASHEK Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children in India. Donations made to our organization are used to support our various programs and initiatives. We want to ensure transparency regarding our refund policy for donations.

2. General Donation Refund Policy

In general, donations to MASHEK Foundation are non-refundable. As a charitable organization, we rely on donations to plan and implement our programs. Once a donation is made, it is immediately put to use in supporting our mission.

3. Exceptions for Refunds

Despite our general no-refund policy, we understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. We will consider refund requests in the following situations:

  • Duplicate Donations: If you have accidentally made a duplicate donation, we will refund one of the donations upon verification.
  • Technical Errors: If a technical error resulted in an incorrect donation amount or multiple unintended donations, we will issue a refund after investigation.
  • Unauthorized Transactions: If you believe that a donation was made using your financial information without your authorization, please contact us immediately.

4. Timeframe for Refund Requests

All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the donation date. Requests made after this period may not be eligible for a refund.

5. Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at mashek.org@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Date of donation
  • Amount donated
  • Payment method used
  • Reason for requesting a refund
  • Any relevant transaction details or receipts

6. Processing Time

We will review refund requests as quickly as possible. If approved, refunds will typically be processed within 10-15 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

7. Product Purchases

For merchandise purchased through our online store:

  • Return Period: Items may be returned within 14 days of receipt if they are in unused condition with original packaging.
  • Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of receipt with photos of the damage/defect.
  • Return Shipping: Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is damaged or defective.
  • Refund Processing: Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 7 business days.

8. Event Registrations

Refunds for event registrations are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made 15-30 days before the event are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days before the event are not eligible for a refund.
  • If MASHEK Foundation cancels an event, registrants will receive a full refund.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website.
