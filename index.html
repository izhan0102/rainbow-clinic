Goals

At Mashek, we're committed to creating lasting positive change across multiple areas of society. Our holistic approach addresses the interconnected challenges facing communities across India.

Education

Providing quality education to underprivileged children through free learning centers, scholarships, and educational resources.

Peace Building

Fostering harmony and understanding through community dialogues, youth leadership programs.

Healthcare

Improving access to essential healthcare services through medical camps, nutrition programs, and health education initiatives.

Women Empowerment

Enabling women to become economically independent through skills training, entrepreneurship support, and advocacy programs.

Entrepreneurship

Nurturing the next generation of social entrepreneurs through mentorship, seed funding, and business development support.

About MASHEK

Established in 2017, MASHEK (NGO) is engaged in improving the lives of underprivileged children by bringing about a positive change in their lives by providing them with necessary education, health facilities & Scholarships. We, at MASHEK (NGO), strive to provide education to children who don't have access to formal education mainly by providing them with Free Education, Healthcare, Nutrition, Scholarships, etc.
