Audiology Services

Comprehensive hearing care for your child

Complete Audiological Assessment & Rehabilitation

Our clinic offers comprehensive audiological services delivered by experienced professionals using state-of-the-art equipment. We provide accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for children with hearing and balance disorders.

Pure tone audiometry (PTA)

Assessment of hearing sensitivity across different frequencies to determine hearing thresholds.

Speech Audiometry (SRT/SDT)

Evaluation of speech reception thresholds and speech discrimination abilities.

Special tests (SISI/TONE DECAY/ABLB)

Specialized auditory tests to diagnose specific hearing disorders.

Tympanometry

Assessment of middle ear function and eardrum mobility.

Hearing Aid Trial and Fitting

Selection, fitting, and fine-tuning of appropriate hearing aids for children.

Cochlear Implant Rehabilitation

Post-implantation therapy to maximize the benefits of cochlear implants.

Vestibular Assessment and Rehabilitation

Diagnosis and treatment of balance disorders in children.

Auditory Evoked Potential Tests (ABR, ASSR)

Objective hearing tests to assess auditory pathway function.

Speech and Language Therapy Excellence

Our speech and language therapy services address a wide range of communication disorders. We work with children of all ages to improve their speech, language, fluency, and social communication skills through evidence-based interventions.

About Rainbow

Our team of autism specialists creates a nurturing, structured environment where children with autism feel safe, understood, and supported in their unique development journey.
