About Rainbow

At Rainbow Clinic, we believe every child deserves to thrive mentally and emotionally.

Our team of specialized child psychologists creates a nurturing and playful environment where children feel safe, understood, and empowered.

Located in Rajbagh, our "Subhuk Gaash" Rainbow Clinic truly serves as "A Ray of Hope" for children and families seeking professional psychological support.

Certified Professionals

All our therapists are certified child psychology specialists.

Child-Centered Approach

We prioritize each child's unique needs and experiences.

Safe Environment

Our clinic provides a secure and comfortable space for healing.

Flexible Scheduling

We offer convenient appointment times to fit your schedule.

Family Involvement

We collaborate with families throughout the therapeutic process.

Progress Tracking

We regularly evaluate and communicate your child's development.
